## Purpose
Define the initial steps to validate and authorize work, align stakeholders, and create a lightweight plan.

## When to use
Whenever a new project idea or feature proposal is ready to be explored.

## Goals
- Confirm business need and measurable outcome
- Identify stakeholders & champions, making sure Stakeholder Rep, Scrum Master, UX Designer, Technical Architect, and other personas are listed as relevant
- Define success criteria and initial timeline
- Decide go/no-go for planning

## Minimum Deliverables
- Project One-pager (Problem, Goal, Success Metrics)
- Stakeholder list & communication plan (explicitly list Stakeholder Rep and key roles)
- High-level timeline and key milestones
- Initial risk list (risk owner can be PM, Scrum Master, Tech Architect, etc.)
- Resource needs (team roles, effort estimate includes all personas)

## Initiation Checklist
- [ ] One-pager completed and reviewed by Product Lead and Scrum Master
- [ ] Sponsor / Stakeholder alignment (meeting includes Stakeholder Rep)
- [ ] Decision: Approve to move into planning?
- [ ] Create repo or project board skeleton
- [ ] Add initial artifacts to repo (docs/ or .copilot/)
- [ ] Confirm new roles/personas are represented

## Decision Gate
Move to planning when:
- Success metrics are clear
- Stakeholders agree on priority (Stakeholder Rep validates)
- Team availability (all needed personas) is confirmed
